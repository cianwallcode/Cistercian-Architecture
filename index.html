Architectural Elements

The Cloister
Central to the monastic spatial logic, providing the primary circulation and meditative zone.
The Roof
Evolutionary progression from simple timber framing to more complex stone vaulting.
The Lancet Window
A primary indicator of the "Early English" style adopted during the 13th century.
The Entrance
Transitional portals moving from Romanesque rounded arches to the pointed Gothic arch.
